## Notes

- **OPCODES** — Raw bytecode execution throughput
- **PATHS** — Symbolic path exploration speed
- **SOLVING** — Z3 constraint solving latency (higher = harder problems)
- **MEMORY** — State forking and copy-on-write efficiency
- **CONCURRENCY** — Thread interleaving and race detection speed

The `incremental_solver` and `constraint_hashing` benchmarks are intentionally
slow — they test Z3 under heavy constraint load (many variables, nested
quantifiers). This is expected behaviour for an SMT-based verifier.

## Reproducing
